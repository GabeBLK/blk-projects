About Us

BLK Projects is a Sydney-based carpentry and maintenance company specialising in carpentry and general maintenance services. With over 12 years of industry experience, we deliver reliable, compliant, and high-quality workmanship across every project.

We work closely with strata managers, government bodies, and insurance providers, ensuring all works are completed to the required standards and compliance requirements. Our focus is on professionalism, attention to detail, and delivering dependable outcomes our clients can trust.
